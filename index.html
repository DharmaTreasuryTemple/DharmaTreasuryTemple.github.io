​American Mahayana Buddhism in the silicon valley

Dharma Treasury Temple is the 6th branch temple of BLI.  Founded by Master YongHua, BLI implements the vision of predecessor of Mahayana Buddhism. Our American Mahayana Buddhism help followers cope with stress, improve health, become more compassionate, and develop bigger and deeper resolve for societal contributions.

​​​Services the community of sunset district and northern california

Dharma Treasury Temple offers meditation class, weekend ceremony, Dharma talks and vegetarian lunch to the community every weekend, free of charge. The temple also hosts events through out the year that are open to the community.
About Us

Meet the Abbot of Dharma Treasury Temple

Master XianZhi became the abbot of Dharma Treasury Temple in 2023. Prior to this he was the abbot of Jewel Mountain Temple in South Korea.

Master XianZhi left the home life and become a monk under Master YongHua in 2018. Since then, he has become more grateful of the Dharma and would like to share the benefits with others. 

Many people feel a sense of peace when they draw near Master XianZhi, he is fondly called, "Master Z".
