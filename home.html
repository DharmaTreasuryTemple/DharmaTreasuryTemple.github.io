      We are dedicated to facilitating your self-improvement on your spiritual journey

      At Dharma Treasury Temple, we practise American Maha​yana.

      American Mahayana Buddhism is Chinese Mahayana reinterpreted for the American culture and sensibilities. ​
      ​
      The aim is to help followers in modern day society cope with stress, improve health, become more compassionate, and develop bigger and deeper resolve for societal contributions.

      Meet Dharma Treasury Temple's Leadership Team

      Master YongHua - Founder of Bodhi Light International Inc

      Master YongHua left home life under Great Master XuanHua in 1995 and became ordained under Master Man Giac. He advocates the simultaneous practice of Chan and Pureland, and is dedicated to giving  individualized guidance to help each person find methods that are most appropriate to his/her current cultivation level.​

      Master XianZhi - Abbot of Dharma Treasury Temple

      Fondly known as Master Z to BLI disciples, Master XianZhi was the abbot of Jeweled Mountain Temple in South Korea prior to becoming the abbot of Dharma Treasury Temple in 2023. Master XianZhi left the home life and become a monk under Master YongHua in 2018. Out of gratitude to the Dharma, Master XianZhi is committed to sharing its benefits to more people.
