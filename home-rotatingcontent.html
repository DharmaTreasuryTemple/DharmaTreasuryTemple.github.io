Thor Mantra Session

During the Mediation Retreat, Thor mantra sessions are offered daily from 1 to 2 PM PDT.

The abbot of Dharma Treasury Temple, Master XianZhi, leads the Thor mantra session. His mantra recitation is extremely powerful and efficacious.

​Many reported getting great benefits from attending his mantra sessions, and have become regulars of his mantra sessions.
