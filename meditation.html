​Meditation at Japanese Tea Garden

Wednesday 9 AM

Japanese Tea Garden
75 Hagiwara Tea Garden Drive San Francisco, CA 94118

Join us for a peaceful morning at the oldest public Japanese Garden in North America. Surrounded by the serene beauty of Japanese maples, pines, azaleas, and cherry trees, we'll gather at the tea shop for group meditation. Whether you're new or experienced in meditation, this is a great chance to deepen your practice. Our local Sangha from Dharma Treasury Temple (Sunset District) will guide the session and be available for any physical or spiritual questions.
After meditation, you're welcome to stay and explore the garden with us. (Look for the group inside the tea shop.)
Details:
  • Date & Time: Wednesdays in October, 9-10am
  • Location: Japanese Tea Garden (75 Hagiwara Tea Garden Drive)
  • Cost: Free (with a required purchase of food or drink at the tea shop)
  • Parking: Free street parking around Golden Gate Park
  • Contact: 510-557-8324 (Buddhist monk XianLai)
We look forward to sharing this peaceful experience with you!
Note for non-residents: Free entrance to the Japanese Tea Garden is available from 9-10am on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday.

Saturday 4 PM

Chain of Lakes Meadow Parking Lot
​371 Chain of Lakes Drive East San Francisco, CA 94122
Explore Golden Gate Park with Local Buddhist Monks & Learn Meditation
Bring your friends and family—no RSVP needed for additional guests.
HIKE&MEDITATE Golden Gate Park is a beautiful spot for a peaceful afternoon, and we invite you to join us for a unique experience with local Buddhist monks. Explore Golden Gate Park with monks from Dharma Treasury Temple, a branch of Bodhi Light International (BLI). Whether you're new to meditation or an experienced practitioner, our trained teachers will help you build a solid foundation and guide you through challenges. American Chan meditation emphasizes self-improvement and personal growth in all areas of life. With dedication, you’ll deepen your Samadhi (concentration) and notice improvements in mental and physical health, productivity, creativity, relationships, and problem-solving skills.
What to Expect: Meet us at the Chain of Lakes Meadow Parking Lot and embark on a mindful journey with Buddhist monks from the Dharma Treasury Temple. We'll walk from the parking lot to explore this iconic park of San Francisco together. Discover the beauty of various gardens and landscapes together. During the walk, we'll find a serene spot for sitting meditation. We can decide together where to practice meditation.
  • Date: Saturdays 4pm
  • Meeting Point: Chain of Lakes Meadow Parking Lot (371 Chain of Lakes Drive E, S.F.)
  • Parking: Free parking is available.
  • Dog-Friendly: Dogs are welcome, and they are fine for meditation!
Engage with Our Monks
Feel free to ask questions and engage in casual conversations about spiritual practices and meditation with our local Buddhist monks. They are open to discussing their practices and experiences.
